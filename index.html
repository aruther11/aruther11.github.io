PERSONAL DETAILS

I am a Cincinnati born Minneapolis transplant who views coding as one way to escape the brutal winters. I love coding and working with data because it enables you to be at the forefront of key fact based decision making. I currently work as a data analyst and in short, I turn problems into opportunities. I love to hear from a variety of stakeholders, identify the issues, create metrics that will help us keep an eye on the problems, and then manipulate and visualize the data to provide tools that produce insights.

I enjoy providing information on what’s currently happening but I am looking to expand my tool set to enable me to help understand what can happen in the future. Predictive analytics is fascinating and growing part of almost every organization. I am hoping to attend an online grad school and acquire a masters in Data Science to push me further in my career. Take a look at portfolio and see the steps that I have made in order to become a data scientist.

CURRENT SKILLS & RESPONSIBILITIES


KPI Design

I meet with stakeholders to understand their problems and come up with the metrics that will help us identify opportunities for growth. I then work with the technical subject matter experts that know where we can find the data points needed to create the metrics.


ETL

I write complex queries to manipulate the raw data into the metrics needed to provide insights and store the clean metrics into tables that are ready to be used for dashboards. At my current job I use a combination of SQL Server, Postgres, and Python to complete these tasks.


Data Visualization

Once the data is clean the true fun begins. I love to use Tableau as my dashboard tool of choice and SSRS to give users alerts and numbers directly to their emails at certain times throughout the day. While Tableau is great it has it’s limitations. I hope to get better at web based visualization tools to be able to turn any visualization design into reality.
